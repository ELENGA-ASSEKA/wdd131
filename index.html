Republic of Congo, The Republic of the Congo (also known as Congo-Brazzaville) is a country located in Central Africa, bordered by Gabon to the west, Cameroon and the Central African Republic to the north, the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the east and south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the southwest. Its capital and largest city is Brazzaville. With a population of around 5.5 million people, the Republic of the Congo is rich in natural resources, particularly oil, which is the backbone of its economy.
